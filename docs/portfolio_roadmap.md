# Portfolio Restructure Roadmap for Preet Viradiya

## Goal

Rebuild the portfolio from an early student portfolio into a recruiter-facing AI systems portfolio. The new site should position Preet as an AI Systems / ML Engineer with strength in agentic AI, Video-RAG, edge AI, real-time inference, MLOps, and client-facing AI delivery.

## Current state audit

The current live website is simple but outdated:
- Hero says: “Machine Learning Engineer and a Software developer.”
- Experience section only shows B.Tech at Amity University.
- Portfolio highlights older student projects like BTHS and AI hand controller.
- Virtual internships and certificates occupy too much space.
- It does not show University of Toronto MScAC, JSquared, Sapiient, TechNomads, production AI systems, agentic AI, Video-RAG, inference optimization, MLOps, or business metrics.
- Footer still says © 2021.
- Contact/public handles are icon-based and not recruiter-optimized.
- The site has almost no evidence hierarchy: recruiters cannot quickly understand current role, outcomes, project depth, or target role fit.

## New positioning

Primary headline:
AI Systems Engineer building production-grade Agentic AI, Edge/Video AI, RAG, and ML infrastructure.

Alternate headline:
Machine Learning Engineer focused on real-time AI systems, agentic workflows, multimodal retrieval, and high-performance inference.

Recruiter one-liner:
I build production AI systems that connect models, infrastructure, and business workflows—from edge video analytics and Video-RAG to MLOps, inference optimization, and agentic AI systems.

## Homepage structure

### 1. Hero
Show name, high-impact role, recruiter pitch, and CTAs.
Include:
- View Case Studies
- Download Resume
- Contact Me

### 2. Proof metrics
Use metric cards:
- 45% reduced manual monitoring
- 30 FPS real-time video analytics
- 2x throughput improvement
- 60% latency reduction
- 50% lower data acquisition costs
- 30% faster deployments

### 3. Featured case studies
Feature 3–4 strongest projects:
- Production Edge Agentic AI for Mining Safety
- Video-RAG for Continuous Video Search
- Enterprise AI for Sprint Planning and Resource Allocation
- High-Performance Inference and MLOps

### 4. Experience snapshot
Show JSquared, Sapiient, TechNomads with concise bullets and metrics.

### 5. Skills grouped by recruiter search intent
Group by:
- Agentic AI / GenAI / RAG
- ML Systems / Inference Optimization
- Computer Vision / Video AI
- MLOps / Cloud / Data Infrastructure
- Consulting / Product / Client Delivery

### 6. Research and writing
Use this to show depth:
- Human-AI collaboration
- Enterprise AI workflow redesign
- Multimodal video retrieval
- Edge AI for safety-critical systems

### 7. Contact CTA
Make it obvious:
- Email
- LinkedIn
- GitHub
- Resume download

## Final portfolio standard

The site should answer these questions in under 30 seconds:
- Who is Preet?
- What roles is he targeting?
- What hard systems has he built?
- What results has he delivered?
- What technologies does he use?
- Where can a recruiter download the resume or contact him?
